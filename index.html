The Tile Set The tile set is made up of 148 tiles. These 148 tiles are broken into suits that contain 136 total playable tiles, flower suit tiles that contain 8 tiles, and animal suit tiles that contain 4 tiles.

The Bamboo, Circle, and Numbers suits. There are 36 tiles each of the Circle, Bamboo, and Numbers Suit. Each one of these suits has numerical tiles ranging from one to nine containing 4 tiles for each number in the suit. These tiles are shown below.

The Dragon Tiles. There are three unique tiles within the dragon suit with each unique tile having four tiles each for a total of 12 tiles. Each unique tile is referred to as the Green Dragon, Red Dragon, and White Dragon.
